1. Introduction

Welcome to Swap Crab, your one-stop solution for converting BNB to Solana. Swap Crab leverages the power of blockchain technology to provide a secure and efficient bridge between Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and Solana.

2. Getting Started

  • Connect Your Wallet

    • To use Swap Crab, you need to connect your wallet. Follow these steps:

    1. Ensure you have a BSC-compatible wallet like MetaMask installed.
    2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button on the Swap Crab interface.
    3. Approve the wallet connection request.

  • Minimum BNB Amount

    • Before initiating a swap, make sure your BNB amount meets the minimum required threshold. Adjust the amount accordingly.

  • Setting Solana Destination Address

Set the Solana destination address where you want to receive the converted funds. Use the "Destination Address" field for this purpose.

3. Swapping Process

  • Initiating the Swap

    1. Enter the desired BNB amount in the "Amount" field.
    2. Confirm the Solana destination address.
    3. Click the "Swap" button to initiate the transaction.

  • Transaction Confirmation

    • Once the transaction is initiated, wait for confirmation. This may take a few moments. You will receive a confirmation message on the interface.

  • Transaction Hash

After successful confirmation, you will be provided with a transaction hash. Use this hash to track the status of your transaction on the blockchain.
